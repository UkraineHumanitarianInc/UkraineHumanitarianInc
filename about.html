About Us !

Hello!

Marc Zarych.

Since April 2022, my brother John Zarych and I have been supporting the Ukrainian people by traveling to Poland and Ukraine. We started by driving refugees from the border into Poland and soon began personally delivering humanitarian aid with the help of Ukrainian and foreign volunteers all over Ukraine - from Kyiv to Sloviansk, Izyum to Kramatorsk, Zaporizhzhia to Chernihiv. Our New Jersey registered 501(c)(3) organization, Ukraine Humanitarian Inc., has been at the heart of our efforts, making a real difference through donations and our own personnel funds during the ongoing Russian invasion. We aspire to bring hope and support to countless people in Ukraine during this terrible time.
